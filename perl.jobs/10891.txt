Description:
Senior Software Engineer – NetMRI Sustaining Team
Annapolis, MD

Summary: 

This senior software engineer plays a key role for the NetMRI Engineering
Team by providing expert code-level knowledge, troubleshooting and
debugging support to our frontline Customer Support engineers and
implementing permanent software fixes and patches as required.	This
engineer solves challenging system and network interoperability problems in
real customer deployments, and drives the results of his or her first-hand
product experience directly back into our product design process.  The
individual being sought is an experienced software developer who has worked
with UI and server side technologies and is a highly motivated
self-starter, with a strong background in software development and
systematic troubleshooting, combined with a solid understanding of network
products, architectures, and protocols.  The successful candidate will work
on a state of the art LAMP-based software platform that includes web
technologies such as Ruby on Rails, AJAX and ExtJS toolkit.

Key Responsibilities:

•	Respond to requests for assistance from the Customer Support team
and own the Engineering team’s response.
•	Debug the root cause of assigned software defects and implement
permanent, reliable and timely fixes in production code.
•	Improve the company’s abilities to support the product by
developing new support tools and enhancements to support processes and the
product itself.
•	Work closely with other engineering teams to provide feedback on
customer experience and suggest product improvements.
•	Provide input to QA to create and enhance system and performance
test plans based on the types of issues raised in the field.

Requirements:

•	Knowledge and Experience:
o	6+ years of distinguished career in a software development role
with demonstrated experience in design and development of network products,
especially web-based network management applications or OSSs.
o	Strong domain knowledge in enterprise networking (TCP/IP, SNMP,
routing and switching)
o	Must have excellent object-oriented design and programming skills
in one or more programming languages such as Perl, C++, Ruby, PHP or Java.
o	Must have experience in writing optimized SQL queries, especially
for MySQL.
o	System Administrator level experience with Unix (Linux strongly
preferred)
o	Development experience in JavaScript or similar UI side languages
and Ruby on Rails or similar frameworks for web application development.

•	Education
o	Four year degree in CS, CE, EE or other technical field such as
math, physics or engineering is required. 
o	Masters degree in CS/CE is a plus.
o	Networking professional certification such as CCIE or CCNA is
highly desirable.

•	Skills and Abilities:
o	Driven to produce the best results for our customers.
o	Structured and analytical troubleshooting skills applicable to many
domains.
o	Strong time management and organizational skills.
o	Ability to triage and multi-task.
o	Excellent verbal and written communication skills. 
o	Thrive in a fast paced environment and meet stretch goals


Please submit your resume to careers@infoblox.com and include the position
title in the subject line.

Infoblox is an Equal Opportunity Employer M/F/V/D.
