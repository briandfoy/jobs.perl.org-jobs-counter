About DB Global Technology:

DB Global Technology (DBGT) is the newly established North American
development center in Cary, North Carolina. It is located near the Research
Triangle Park (RTP) area of North Carolina. As a subsidiary of Deutsche
Bank AG, DBGT is a dedicated application development and software
engineering center for leading-edge technology solutions that power the
future of global capital markets. DBGT partners with the Deutsche Bank
Group Technology and Operations division to access superior technology
skills and promote innovation in the development of software applications
and IT solutions.

Group Overview

Enterprise Platform Engineering (EPE) is part of the wider Application
Services group that focuses on delivering enterprise services and change
programs across Deutsche Bank and we are in the process of building out a
strong and dynamic engineering team in Cary.

Application and Web Platforms (AWP) is responsible for engineering our Web
and JEE application hosting platforms within EPE. These services are
designed to run a large numbers of applications within the Bank in a highly
available, cost effective and secure shared infrastructure environment.  We
encourage a customer self service paradigm allowing our customers to
maintain their deployments via a unified administration portal, which also
spans several other services.

We utilize a product driven approach where we provide these services to our
clients, the application development teams, in a manner similar to a
managed hosting service provider.  AWP team work closely with Product
Management and the Production Support organization that operate the
platforms we deliver.
