Description:
The Production Operations Engineer will be working in the Production
Operations team which is part of the Business Information Group. The
Business Information Group is responsible for capturing, processing,
storing and delivering the revenue and analytical data for the internal and
external users.  As a member of the Production Operations team, you will be
responsible for providing the necessary support to ensure that Production
data stream is getting processed accurately and promptly in order to meet
the pre-defined SLA (service Level Agreement) timelines. This would involve
monitoring the system very closely. You would be carrying a pager and
serving as a part of Production Support rotation with other team members.
Due to the large volume of data and critical timelines, the pages need to
be attended to promptly and the issues resolved pro-actively. 

Responsibilities:

•	The primary responsibility of this position is to help ensure
continuous, 24X7X365 operation of our data processing and meet the strict
company SLAs.  Meeting this objective requires quick, effective and
efficient troubleshooting of Production Data Stream alerts/issues with
extremely minimal disruption to business operations.

•	Work in co-ordination with senior team members in finding ways to
improve the current system.

•	Communicate closely with the Development and QA teams to optimize
and performance-tune the existing system.
