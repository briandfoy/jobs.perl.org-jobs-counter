Senior Engineer - Ad Server 

Opportunity:

We are looking for an extremely capable individual who has a strong
background in back-end systems development and has applied their knowledge
and experience in ad serving and targeting technology. You will be working
on a team that is combining real-time search and data aggregation
techniques that are used by millions of people worldwide. This is a unique
opportunity for someone who would love to contribute to the development of
new and innovative ad products.


Responsibilities:

•	Work closely with the Product and Development teams to define and
prioritize product features and enhancements. 
•	Assess the feasibility, effort, costs, and timelines for
development as appropriate. 
•	Analyze, design, develop, test, troubleshoot and document complex
data systems that may involve text analysis, content analysis, link/graph
analysis to generate meta-data useful to the targeting engine 
•	Create technical specifications and drive issues to resolution
incorporating current standards and best practices. 
•	Work closely with the Operations Team in the definition of standard
run book policies, monitoring, and management of the systems you develop. 
•	Colloborate in the design of the operating tools to ensure high
availability, accuracy, and reliability of the systems 


Requirements:

•	5+ years of experience in software or algorithm design, preferably
in the areas of ad serving and targeting or distributed systems 
•	3+ years coding in C/C++ and/or Java 
•	Demonstrated experience in a clustered server environment 
•	Strong software architecture background 
•	Experience with the development of a search engine, text analysis,
or related fields 
•	Theoretical and practical background in ad serving and targeting
technology 
•	Exceptional software design and programming skills 
•	Strong foundation in distributed system architecture and
development 
•	Experience with content analysis 
•	Experience with: Linux, Java, REST, XML, Linux, RSS aggregation,
BDB, MySQL 
•	Direct experience with large-scale ad serving and targeting systems
is desirable 
•	BS in Computer Science or equivalent; an MS or Ph.D. is a plus.
