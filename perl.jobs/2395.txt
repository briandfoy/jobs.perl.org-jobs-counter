Do you refuse to take things for granted, preferring instead to subject
hypotheses and opinions to scientific tests?  Do you like figuring out what

ways of generating meaningful answers to them automatically?  The Yahoo!
Search Technology group is seeking an experienced software engineer to join
its Automated Metrics and Process Tools team.  We’re passionate about
building the best search engine on the planet, and our team brings together
some of the best and brightest minds from across the search industry.  Our
product now powers hundreds of millions of queries a day, and the Automated
Metrics and Process Tools team plays a key role in informing decisions
about its direction.  As part of the team, you will be responsible for
designing and implementing automated processes for measuring Search Engine
performance.  You will work with the Relevance Tools, Analysis, and other
Yahoo! Search Technology teams to help maximize the efficiency of our
development and testing processes.  You will play an important part in the
design and building of systems that help improve our ability to measure our
product’s performance and improve the processes behind it.

Essential Responsibilities
Collaborate across Yahoo! Search development groups to help define
meaningful Web Search product metrics and design processes that generate
them automatically and reliably.  Apply statistical methods to analyze the
performance of various subsystems behind Web Search.  Develop robust
database applications with a strong web-based UI component that tell the
product’s story, summarizing complicated metrics in clear and meaningful
ways.  Effectively design, develop, and deploy tools and applications based
on open-source technologies such as Linux, Apache, MySQL, Perl and PHP.
