Description:
About the Job

As a Senior Software Quality Engineer in Akamai’s Enterprise Cloud
Engineering team, you will be responsible for testing and verification of
software components and features that will power Akamai’s Edge Application
Network.

We are seeking an experienced Senior Software Quality Assurance Engineer
with proven distributed systems experience and automation skills to work
with the engineering and quality teams to build system-level test
automation and execute automated and manual tests. An ideal candidate will
have distributed system testing experience, strong analytical abilities,
excellent teamwork and communication skills, and the ability to design,
implement, maintain and extend effective system test automation solutions.

About the Team

You will be part of a highly skilled software engineering team responsible
for the software that powers a scalable, highly distributed fault tolerant
network of servers that hosts virtual machines running a variety of
applications at the Akamai Edge. The software will interface with various
elements of Akamai’s world-class infrastructure such as mapping, customer
care systems, and the Akamai Network Operations Command Center. By
leveraging our global deployment of networks, Akamai can uniquely solve
problems and exceed fundamental limitations of traditional networking.

Responsibilities:
- Work closely with developers, DevQA, SQA Engineers and others to design,
develop, implement and improve test methodologies, test plans and highly
effective test automation.
- Perform manual and automated functional and full system tests for
Akamai’s Enterprise Cloud Products.
- Execute tests on a large-scale distributed test network.
- Create, deploy and maintain automated system tests to replicate
real-world scenarios.
- Review bug descriptions, functional requirements and design documents,
incorporating this information into test plans and test cases.

Required skills:
Basic Qualifications
- Education: Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science or equivalent
experience.
- Overall Experience: Minimum of 5 years experience in software quality
and/or software development engineering experience.
- Minimum of 5 years experience in server-side SQA and/or software
development engineering experience.
- Minimum of 3 years experience testing and/or developing distributed
applications/services.
- Minimum of 3 years experience working with UNIX/Linux operating system.
- Minimum of 3 years experience working with one of more common scripting
languages such as perl, python, ruby, php, and/or shell.
- Minimum of 3 years experience developing and/or executing back-end system
test automation.
- Minimum of 3 years experience working with one or more common Internet
protocols such as IP, TCP, HTTP, DNS, FTP, and/or SSL.

Desired skills:
Desired Qualifications
- Education: Master’s Degree or greater in Computer Science.
- Excellent oral and written communications skills.
- Strong experience developing and maintaining test automation scripts,
libraries and frameworks (OO methodologies strongly preferred, OO Perl

- Experience with Linux internals.
- Experience with client/server testing.
- Experience testing large-scale, large-volume and highly distributed
systems.
- Experience with/knowledge of hypervisor technologies (such as KVM,

- Highly driven, positive attitude, team player, self-motivated and very

- Strong analytical skills, attention to detail and excellent problem
