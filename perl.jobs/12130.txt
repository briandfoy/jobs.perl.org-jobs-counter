SAY is a global media company that helps passionate, independent content
creators publish, build an audience, and earn a living.

Senior Software Engineer – Delivery 
 
At the center of a changing media world, a new breed of independent
creators build audience and shape opinion about the things they love. The
delivery team helps these passionate voices monetize their content by
building a world-class ad delivery system that smartly optimizes the ads in
real time that get to the page. Build the code that drives a large
distributed cluster of servers, which deliver ads that reach 400MM+ people
monthly. The application has to meet both large volume and tight latency
requirements. If you’re looking for a challenging opportunity to work at
high scale we’d love to hear from you. Come join an incredibly talented
team comprised of top engineers from SAY Media.

Responsibilities:

This engineer will need to work broadly across the ad delivery server, as
you’ll be primarily responsible for:
- Take overall responsibility for coding algorithms, framework design, and
backend feature development of the delivery application 
- Implement new ad targeting and delivery features, such as the ability to
better target ads based on end-user attributes 
- Partner with product management to think of creative technical solutions
that allow for the optimal monetization of each page 
- Work with the data team to make sure the server generates the correct
reporting data 
- Partner with mobile team to ensure the server offloads as much work as
possible to maximize delivery to handset devices 
- Draw from extensive experience to design and implement the application
for both large -scale applications with high availability 
- Work with the client engineers and operations team to troubleshoot
production ad delivery issues. 
- Like many current team members you may have an opportunity to contribute
to opensource/CPAN projects.  If you have any publicly available code, we'd
love to check it out!

- Adaptable senior contributor drawing on extensive experience, who works

- Experience with Perl, MySQL, Memcached, Git, Perlbal, Gearman, and open

- Shows initiative through open-source contributions and personal projects
