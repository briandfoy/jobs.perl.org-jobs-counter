Description:
We are looking for a Software Engineer within R&D to develop high quality,
innovative software products and services for MessageLabs, from
specifications and requirements defined by Technical Architects and
Technical Leads. 

The R&D department of MessageLabs is responsible for building the
technology that underpins the MessageLabs business.  This includes the
services / products offered to our customers today: (Anti virus, Anti spam,
Content Control, Image Control & Managed Encryption) as well as new
services that will broaden the portfolio of offerings we can provide to our
customers in the future.

PRINCIPAL ACCOUNTABILITIES:

1. Contributing to the maintenance of MessageLabs Services SLAs by
maintaining and developing systems and tools to meet those objectives.
2. Producing design specifications as required from functional
specifications, identifying all sub tasks, estimating their duration and
feeding back to the Technical Architect, Technical Lead and Project
Manager.  
3. Participating in planning, providing specific estimates and
justification for own areas of work.
4. Completing all tasks as defined and prioritised in the project plan,
ensuring all code is written, tested and integrated with the rest of the
project code base and version control system.
5. Responsible for working with the System Test Engineers throughout the
project lifecycle, Writing detailed instructions on installation & rollback
procedures
6. Investigating bug reports, fixing defects and re-releasing the fixed
software to System Test Engineers.
7. Writing, monitoring and revising all installation, configuration and
support documentation and ensuring that this is up to date at all times.
8. Provide post release support in all instances, including training where
necessary to internal customers, and occasionally with external customers.
