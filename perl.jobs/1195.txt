Description:
Located in Radnor, Pennsylvania, on Philadelphia’s ‘Main Line’, Stevens
Capital Management LP (“SCM”) is a financial consulting firm specializing
in developing and implementing computer-based statistical trading systems
and sophisticated investment strategies.  SCM operates 24 hours a day on
global financial markets.

SCM seeks a bright, experienced individual to support and develop the
company technical infrastructure.  Although the company is relatively
small, the infrastructure is cutting edge and your role will be all
encompassing.  You will provide a lead role in systems support and provide
technical expertise in systems integration and implementation.

Sr. LINUX System Administrator

Join a 5 member administration team responsible for managing a mixed
network running Solaris and Redhat Linux on Sun and Compaq
servers/workstations.  As this environment continues to grow, and more
applications are ported onto Linux, we seek a Sr. Linux admin to work on
all aspects of administering a mixed Solaris/Linux mission critical 24x7
trading environment.  The user base comprises 20 technical and 20
non-technical users, and you would work closely with the real-time trading
systems developers and end-users.  Your responsibilities would include
traditional administration responsibilities to advanced data warehousing,
firewalls, networking, performance tuning, optimization and application
administration issues.	 

Top project and challenge for next 6 months is a new office environment
build out.  Be involved in a complete build out of the unix/linux
infrastructure.
	
Primary responsibilities:
-Administer Compaq Redhat 7- 9.x,/AS and Sun Solaris 8 workstations/servers
running in a 24x7 environment.
-Configure and install new machines – Compaq Xeon/Itanium 2 and Sun
UltraSparc3.
-Basic Linux/Unix administration – kickstart/jumpstart server builds, nfs,
nis, shell scripting, performance tuning, and end-user support.
-Implement software and hardware RAID solutions, including Veritas Volume
Manager, Solaris Volume Manager, Compaq smart arrays, StorEdge T3’s and
3510 FC arrays.
-Research, evaluate and implement fault tolerant and HA solutions including
Veritas Cluster Server and Redhat Cluster Manager.   
-Administer Linux/Solaris to Windows connectivity using Samba and Exceed.
-Support Sybase DBA’s system requirements, currently utilizing 20+
Terabytes of disk space.
-Maintain development environment for real-time electronic trading
applications.
	
Shared responsibilities:
-Administer enterprise backup systems running Legato Networker and using
SpectraLogic 64000 tape libraries (AIT3/645 slot).
-Administer firewalls running Rainwall High Availability software with
Checkpoint NG.
-Backup Network administrator on Cisco/Riverstone 10/100/Gigabit Ethernet
LAN and WAN network
-Backup Trade System Administrator on TIBCO real-time financial data
distribution network, and stand-alone screens such as Reuters and
Bloomberg.

Requirements of the Candidate:
-3+ years of hands-on Linux experience supporting sophisticated mission
critical environments.
-Given the mission critical nature of our applications you must have the
ability to work very well under pressure, and keep abreast of current
technology.
-University degree required.   
-Exposure to networking, NT, SQL, Sybase, Perl would be assets.
-TIBCO/Rendezvous and Solaris experience would be a strong plus.
