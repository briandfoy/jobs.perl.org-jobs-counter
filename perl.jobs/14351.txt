Group Overview

Core Engineering is part of the wider Application Services group that

maintain their deployments via a unified administration portal, which also
spans several other services.

clients, the application development teams, in a manner similar to a
managed hosting service provider.  AWP team work closely with Product
Management and the Production Support organization that operate the
platforms we deliver.
