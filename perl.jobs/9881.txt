Description:
A bioinformaticist position is available within the Buell lab at Michigan
State University in East Lansing Michigan. The position will provide
bioinformatics support to two projects focused on metabolomic, next
generation sequencing, expression profiling, quantitative genetic, and/or
computational biology approaches to address research questions involving
metabolism of essential nutrients and pharmaceutical compounds in model,
crop, and medicinal plant species. See
http://buell-lab.plantbiology.msu.edu/ and
http://medicinalplantgenomics.msu.edu/ for more information on research
activities in the Buell lab.

The bioinformaticist will be responsible for analyzing and disseminating
genomic data including next generation sequence data. The position is
responsible for maintaining analysis pipelines and quality checking the
results, parsing and formatting data from various sources, converting data
formats, ensuring scalability of pipelines on large data sets in a
distributed computing environment, using NCBI and other online
bioinformatics databanks. Other tasks include creation of web sites,
dynamic web pages and tools, and maintaining existing programs and
pipelines.


Required skills:
Minimum Requirements: A Bachelor’s degree in Biology, Bioinformatics,
Computer Science or a related field; Experience with software specific to
the area of research including experience with UNIX or a UNIX-like platform
(Linux, Mac OS X, Solaris, AIX); bioinformatics programs; scripting
languages: Perl, Ruby, or Python; programming languages: C, C++, or Java;
web development: PHP or CGI, HTML, CSS; relational database management
system: MySQL, Postgres, Sybase, or Oracle; or an equivalent combination of
education and experience.

Desired skills:
Desired Education/Experience/Skills: Ability to present data (orally and
written).  Ability to contribute to project meetings. Ability to work in a
team environment.  Demonstrated ability to multi-task as required.  Ability
to read scientific literature pertinent to job. Previous experience in
bioinformatics and genomics.
