Company Overview

Symantec is a global leader in providing security, storage and systems
management solutions to help our customers – from consumers and small

information-driven world against more risks at more points, more completely
and efficiently. Our software and services protect completely, in ways that
can be easily managed and with controls that can be enforced automatically

Department Overview

Symantec Hosted Services leverage Symantec infrastructure to deliver
subscription-based services online. There's no software for customers to
install and no upgrades to manage. All offerings are ‘multi-tenant',
meaning a single instance of the offering is used by all customers at the
same time.   Symantec’s Hosted Services include Symantec Protection Network
- our software-as-a-service (SaaS) platform for providing Symantec
technologies to customers in the cloud as online services.  DeepSight Early
Warning Services  provides actionable security intelligence to customers
using alerts and notifications based on data feeds from the Global
Intelligence Network.
