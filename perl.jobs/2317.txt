Description:
Purpose:
Programming and database management for bioinformatics group and computer /
intranet / systems support companywide.

Duties:
Design and implement computer scripts for bioinformatics analyses. 
Examples of daily work include database downloading and manipulation,
comparative genomics, sequence alignments and assembly, support of
molecular biology cross species gene discovery.  Most scripting is done in
Perl and examples of programs used include NCBI-BLAST and HMMER/Pfam.  The
individual will be an integral member of our target curation team. 
Maintain intranet and associated MYSQL databases.  Administer Linux
(bioinformatics) servers and windows (user) computers; includes
responsibility for security audits, backups, user support.  


Required skills:
Minimum requirements are either a bachelors or masters degree in the
biological sciences with a solid scripting background, or a bachelors or
masters degree in computer science with a solid molecular biology
background. One year's experience in a directly applicable job is
preferred.  (Ph.D. level candidates will also be considered though
responsibilities may differ from the description.)  
For bioinformatics scripting, the candidate must be fluent in Perl, Unix
Shell and compiler scripting and the maintenance of MYSQL databases. 
Familiarity with BLAST is required and knowledge of HMMER/Pfam, NCBI
toolkit and BioPerl modules, Filemaker and ACeDB databases, and NCBI and
model organism databases is advantageous as is a working knowledge of
molecular biology and genomics.  For intranet scripting, knowledge of HTML,
XML, perl-cgi, apache server administration, and PHP is advantageous. 
Experience with Linux, Windows 2000 and Windows XP operating systems is
also required and prior system administration experience is advantageous. 
The successful candidate will be dependable, organized, hard-working, a
self-starter, goal-oriented, have good written and oral communication
skills, and attention to detail.  The ability to handle multiple tasks and
priorities is essential.
