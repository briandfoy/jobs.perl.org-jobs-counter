relevant experience in the field of Bioinformatics, be computer literate in
a unix environment and have an excellent knowledge of database design and
optimisation (preferably in MySQL), programming skills and Perl and OO
Perl, XML, XSD, Javascript, AJAX, Experience in web services, particularly
RESTful and/or DAS and understand the principles of web security.

Knowledge of standard bioinformatics tools, production software
development, ontologies, Catalyst and DBIx::Class  and protein biochemistry
are desirable, but not essential.
