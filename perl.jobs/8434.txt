Description:
Purpose of Position
The technical architect will guide the architecture, design, and
implementation of new components for ED PulseCheck and other applications
used in hospitals. The architect will be a key contributor and needs to be
able to provide high quality conceptual work and guidance as well as
deliver hands-on development work. The architect must have senior-level
experience working with all aspects of web-based application development in
different environments. The candidate must have experience in high level
language programming, like C# and Perl as well as deep knowledge of
database concepts. Excellent communication skills are required to convey
the concepts to business stakeholders, analysts, and developers.


Summary of Responsibilities 

•	Lead the architectural effort for new components of Picis’
web-based applications.
•	Design the technical architecture to achieve reusable and flexible
components.
•	Develop, independently and with other engineers, key architectural
components, frameworks and other code.
•	Evaluate and recommend architecture and design for new components.
•	Guide implementation of design and assist in prototyping.
•	Review current architecture and designs, evaluate and propose
potential improvements.
•	Document and present the architectural strategy both internally and
externally.
•	Work with customers to understand their needs and design high level
solutions.
•	Analyze performance and load implications of recommended
architecture.
•	Provide scalability measures and specifications.
•	Participate in system testing and integration of application
software.
•	Lead and coach developers on the architectural team.
•	Study new products and frameworks to anticipate future needs.
•	Work with Product Management to understand product functionality
required.
•	Create and maintain documentation and design specifications to meet
customer needs and regulatory requirements.
•	Spend about 25% of the time on communication.

•	Minimum of eight years of software design and architecture

•	Experience with .NET framework, C#, Perl, SQL, Web Services,
