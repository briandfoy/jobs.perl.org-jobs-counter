Job Description:
---------------
The Senior Production Services Technician position is a great opportunity
for technical individuals with an interest in visual effects and animation
to assist in
the production of feature films. This group is responsible for managing all

data for a particular show, including file backup and retrieval, monitoring

of renders and image processes, and various troubleshooting tasks. 

Duties include:
-------------
Scripting tools in Perl/Python to automate common tasks and improve
workflow. 
Troubleshooting a wide variety of technical problems.
Communication with Digital, Feature Production, Editorial, I/O, Systems and
Software departments.
Render queue management.
File/image processing and manipulation.
Quality control of images displayed in dailies.
Creating reports for dailies, rounds and archiving.
Prepping and loading frames onto high resolution viewing systems.
Monitoring nightly filmouts.
Disk resource management.
Data archival and restoration.
Handling of outside vendor media.
Digital I/O for the Inferno department.


Required skills:
Bachelors degree in a technical field.
Solid Linux or UNIX Operating System experience.
Proven scripting skills (i.e. shell, Perl or Python).
Strong communication, troubleshooting and multitasking abilities.


Desired skills:
Past leadership or managerial-type role.
Previous production experience preferred.
