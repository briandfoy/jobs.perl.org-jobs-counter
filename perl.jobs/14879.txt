Description:
Our organization is providing network infrastructure services to one of the
largest enterprises in the world. In order to manage these services, a
large collection of network management systems and applications needs to be
continuously maintained and expanded. This management suite consists of a
combination of commercially available systems and custom developed
middleware and applications. The middleware provides the glue between
various administrative, network management and business reporting systems.

The custom built middleware, and the (web) applications and interfaces
relying on it, consist of a variety of technologies and platforms, but
mainly Apache, MySQL, Perl, LDAP running on Red Hat Enterprise Linux
platforms. Additionally there are also a few systems and applications
relying on Wintel platforms like .Net, SQL Server, Internet Information
Server, Office Communications Server and Active Directory.

The Systems Analyst-Perl developer is providing the knowledge and
experience to work on new and ongoing development projects where above
technologies are used, combined and integrated.

Required skills:
Summary of essential job functions:
• Maintain and create intelligent and robust middleware solutions.
• Work in a team of developers that have varying levels of expertise and
experience.
• Provide and document inputs to technical specifications.
• Provide and document inputs to functional specifications where none
provided.
• Develop systems and applications that support the goals of the
organization according to existing standards and guidelines.
• Participate in system integration and user acceptance testing.
• Share knowledge and experience.

Education:
• Bsc in Computer Sciences or

relating to systems integration and application development.

systems.
	
Experience:
• A minimum of 3 years of system integration experience using (OO) Perl,
MySQL.
• A minimum of 3 years of (web) application development using (OO) Perl,
MySQL/Oracle, CGI, HTML, XML, CSS and JavaScript technology.
• Experience with using a web application framework (Catalyst) is a serious
advantage
• A good understanding of IP network technologies and services like TELNET,
SSH and SNMP is an advantage.
• Knowledge of ITIL processes, especially Problem and Incident Management,
is an advantage.
