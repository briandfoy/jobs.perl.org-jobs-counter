MySQL professional services consultant delivers consulting services to our
customers. The professional services consultant will work with other
professional services team members, customers, partners and
sub-contractors, sales team and engineering group to ensure successful
delivery to our clients.

leverage free software like Perl in their day-to-day efforts.

    * Responsibility for database design, administration, tuning and
optimization, migration from other database vendors to MySQL, development
and integration in support of customer engagements. This includes
accountability for functional completeness and delivery against
performance, supportability, and testability goals.

pre-sale engagements to produce mockups and proof of concepts.
    * Working closely with clients and MySQL project managers and team to
clearly define scope of work and provide accurate schedule estimates.
    * Acting as technical guide and mentor to other team members.
    * Creating and maintaining technical documentation, best practices and
methodology.
    * Translating customer specifications into quality deliverables.
    * Following and enhancing coding standards and development guidelines.
    * Participating in code and design reviews.
