Senior Software Engineer - Delivery
 
At the center of a changing media world a new breed of independent creators
build audience and shape opinion about the things they love. The delivery
team helps these passionate voices monetize their content by building a
world-class ad delivery system that smartly optimizes the ads in real time
that get to the page.
Help build the code that drives a large distributed cluster of servers,
which deliver ads that reach 350MM+ people monthly. The application has to
meet both large volume and tight latency requirements. If you’re looking
for a challenging opportunity to work at high scale we’d love to hear from
you.  Come join the incredibly talented team of engineers at SAY Media
(formerly Six Apart + Video Egg).
 
Qualifications:

* 5+ years developing interactive high-volume web-based systems
* Adaptable senior contributor drawing on extensive experience who works
autonomously and is capable of finding answers from the code or from other
people
* Strong leadership skills and desire to mentor junior engineers
* Experience in a platform or service-oriented environment with many
dependencies and high uptime requirements
* At home in a world of distributed systems at scale:  file and document
storage, caching, locking, job processing, search, load balancing, etc.
* Values a direct and collaborative relationship with Operations teams,
helping to support issues escalated to engineering
* Familiarity with Perl, MySQL, NoSQL, Memcached, Git, Perlbal, Gearman,
and open source infrastructure components
* Familiarity with Python helpful but not required
* Comfortable understanding database performance issues, including MySQL
caching and partitioning
* Experience with ad delivery, optimization and reporting helpful.
* Shows initiative through open-source contributions and personal projects
* Advocate for automated testing and test-driven development practices
* Strong communication skills and desire to learn and grow with a highly
talented engineering team
* BS/CS or equivalent experience

Responsibilities:

* Take overall responsibility for coding algorithms, framework design, and
backend feature development of the delivery application
* Implement new ad targeting and delivery features, such as the ability to
better target ads based on end-user attributes
* Partner with product management to think of creative technical solutions
that allow for the optimal monetization of each page
* Work with the data team to make sure the server generates the correct
reporting data
* Partner with mobile team to ensure the server offloads as much work as
possible to maximize delivery to handset devices
* Draw from extensive experience to design and implement the application
for both large-scale applications with high availability
* Work with the client engineers and operations team to troubleshoot
production ad delivery issues.

Like many current team members you may have an opportunity to contribute to
open source/CPAN projects.  If you have any publicly available code, we'd
love to check it out!
