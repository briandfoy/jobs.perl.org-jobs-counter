Description:
Summary of Position
The Application Developer contributes to the development of web portal
interfaces that allow medical care professionals, systems administrators
and technical support staff to securely communicate with other care
providers, and interact with longitudinal health records.  These are
integrated with other applications in the CareAlign Suite, a
cross-organizational system for sharing medical records. Daily activities
include working with an Agile scrum team of other engineers to design,
develop, test and maintain software components, including user interfaces
and databases.

Required skills:
Qualifications, Functions and Responsibilities

Technical
•	Four-year college degree in computer science, related scientific
degree or an equivalent combination of education and related experience in
software development
•	Two  years of full software lifecycle experience designing,
developing and maintaining applications with limited technical direction
•	Demonstrates a working knowledge of object oriented software
development
•	Able to design, write, and execute reliable test plans
•	Understands design, architecture and implementation of components
•	Makes component design suggestions
•	Demonstrates technical skills specific to the team: Perl, HTML,
CSS, XML, Javascript
•	Provides ongoing input into technical and architectural decisions
•	Exemplifies test-driven development with unit testing frameworks
•	Designs and develops web user interfaces
•	Designs databases and develops with RDBMS systems and ORM utilities

•	Able to deploy and configure servers and software for team needs
such as mail and continuous integration
•	Familiar with mail services like Dovecot, Postfix, and IMAPProxy

Business
•	Has working business knowledge specific to the product the team is
building

Independence
•	Designs, implements, and tests smaller sized stories independently

Leadership
•	Creates tasks associated with user stories, and works with other
developers to ensure stories are completed

Soft Skills
•	Contributes to the success of Scrum team by actively participating
in the product and process feedback loops
•	Writes coherent and grammatically correct bug reproduction steps,
documentation, etc.
•	Communicates progress, roadblocks, etc. with team effectively
•	Illustrates a positive attitude
•	Analyzes & solves problems independently
