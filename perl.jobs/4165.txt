What We Do: 
We develop Perl-based technologies for leading Open Source, high-volume

The Quality Assurance Engineer III is responsible for writing, implementing
and executing manual and automated test cases to exercise our product

data sets. The Quality Assurance Engineer III will be expected to find,
isolate and enter bug reports in a bug database (including difficult to

larger investigations to isolate bugs, and work directly with Development
and Program Management to get bugs fixed. Quality Assurance III will help
design quality assurance processes and related testing activities.

as they are defined.

    * Write and track software defect reports using bug tracking software.
    * Track and communicate test results.
    * Create and update test automation scripts to support test cases and
test plans.
    * Document test and/or test automation process and test approach for
assigned areas of responsibility.
    * Create and maintain QA documentation so it is up-to-date and
accurate.
    * Work with the QA team to maintain a test repository.
    * Provide complete and accurate release notes.
    * Assist with training, mentoring and recruiting as necessary.
    * Work with functional specifications, market requirements documents
and other source materials as needed in support of QA efforts.
