Description:
Who are we?
 
Ticketmaster is a Live Nation Entertainment, Inc. company. Live Nation
Entertainment (NYSE-LYV) is the largest live entertainment company in the
world, consisting of five businesses: concert promotion and venue
operations, sponsorship, ticketing solutions, e-commerce and artist
management. Live Nation seeks to innovate and enhance the live
entertainment experience for artists and fans: before, during and after the
show. In 2009, Live Nation sold 140 million tickets, promoted 21,000
concerts, partnered with 850 sponsors and averaged 25 million unique
monthly users of its e-commerce sites.
 
As the world's leading live entertainment ticketing and marketing company,
Ticketmaster connects the world to live entertainment. We provide ticket
sales, marketing and distribution through retail outlets, worldwide call
centers and www.ticketmaster.com, one of the largest e-commerce sites on
the Internet. Ticketmaster is headquartered in the heart of Hollywood,
California.
 
Who are you?

Passionate about technology and motivated to succeed. Driven, with an
entrepreneurial spirit. Resourceful, innovative, forward thinking and
committed. At Ticketmaster, our people embrace these qualities, so if this
sounds like you then please read on!  
 
The Role:
 
As a Software Engineer, you will be part of a team that works closely with
the Marketing team to develop and maintain the email notification systems
of Ticketmaster. This development team plays a major role in supporting the
various email marketing and alert products of Ticketmaster by building
innovative, high-end, scalable email deployment systems.
 
The Software Engineer will be responsible for developing and enhancing the
email marketing system, recommendation engine, various tools, and email
templates. There will also be operational duties including monitoring,
fixing and troubleshooting our proprietary email generation system.
 
We are looking for candidates who enjoy designing complex technology,
tracking down issues, and have the desire to solve technical problems.
 
The engineer will be required to carry a cell phone and provide on-call
after hours support (on a rotating supporting schedule). 
 
 
Minimum Requirements
 
---------------------------------
 
·	  Must be proficient Unix and SQL.
 
·	  Must be proficient with a scripting language. Perl is strongly
preferred.
 
·	  Experience with a templating framework, preferably Template
Toolkit.
 
·	  Experience with Java, Apache, HTML, Object Oriented Perl or
Hadoop is a plus.
 
·	  Familiarity with Infor Outbound Marketing software is a plus.
 
·	  Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering or
Information Technology or related fields is required.
 
·	  3+ years experience with object oriented software development is
required
