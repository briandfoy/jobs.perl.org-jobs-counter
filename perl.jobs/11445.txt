Description:
POSITION SUMMARY 
The Manager of SE will be responsible to build and manage a team of high
performing systems engineers, supporting a variety of complex systems and
application.This person is responsible for clearly communicating a vision
to the team that defines the team’s purpose, ownership, contribution and
commitment. This person directly oversees, mentors and develops systems
engineers on technical leadership, project management, design engineering,
architecture, problem analysis, customer service, optimization and
collaboration. 

In addition, the Manager of SE will be responsible for technical analysis,
effort estimation, production environment platform/system design,
architectural fit and compliance, resource schedules, delivery milestones,
and overall production environment quality. Will lead a team of engineers
in a fast paced dynamic environment. The manager of SE will be report to
the Senior Manager of SE with responsibilities including career management
and setting the operational direction for areas of ownership. 

The ideal candidate will be a “hands on” top level systems engineer who
will provide senior level technical direction on enterprise level projects.
Exemplary leadership and communication abilities (both verbal and written)
are a must; this role will partner closely with business and technology
leadership in delivering systems which meet and exceed business objectives.


Exemplary interpersonal and communication skills are an absolute must with
a demonstrated ability to work well with both engineering and marketing
colleagues; including the ability to thoughtfully present to senior
executives. Experience owning and resolving issues that impact design,
product success, or address future concepts, products, or technologies.
Disciplined approach to maintaining and enforcing engineering best
practices. Ability to synthesize robust and cost-effective technical
solutions against customer requirements providing buy versus build decision
making. Consistent, compassionate and inspiring manager to the team.
Positive can-do partnership attitude while working with teams,
cross-functional teams and cross-functional business units. Must be detail
orientated, self-organized, and capable of simultaneously tracking multiple
issues of varying complexity. 


Required skills:
QUALIFICATIONS FOR POSITION 
• 3-5 years or more of management experience in an IT/web operations
environment. 
• BS in computer science or other technical discipline, or equivalent
experience if no degree. 
• Knowledge of 1 or more scripting Languages (python, perl, or equivalent) 

• Experience in defining and implementing automated processed and software
to maximize team efficiency. 
• Proven experience with Agile (or similar SDLC methodology) project
management and the ability to mentor reports in Agile best practices. 
• Provide architecture/application integration oversight on behalf of the
team to ensure that the sites/services they manage are highly available,
scalable, and secure 
• Work closely with the BU and CE Eng teams as far upstream as possible to

• Maintain a healthy, open technical dialogue with the Eng teams to ensure

• Represent the team in relevant BU and TSS meetings.

• Manage team ticket/task/goal/priority setting, and tracking. 
• Manage team capacity during discovery and on boarding of new projects to
ensure resource availability.
