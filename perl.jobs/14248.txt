Dominion Resources is looking for a Senior EMS Programmer.  This senior

supporting a vendor (Alstom Grid) based SCADA system used to operate and
monitor the electrical transmission grid and other assets. A broad
knowledge and/or willingness to learn a variety of skills from
configuration, programming, automation, business operations etc. will be
required.
It is essential the successful applicant be a highly motivated self starter

supervision. Works fairly independently, this role formulates or defines

in operation and activities. Candidate works at the highest technical level
on all phases of software systems programming applications. Candidate is
responsible for working with an individual business unit on multi-project
assignments.
