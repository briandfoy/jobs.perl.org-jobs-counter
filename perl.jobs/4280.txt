The Quality Assurance Developer's primary responsibility is to
identify and mitigate software quality issues associated with the
design, production and release of software products.  

 

As a member of the Quality Assurance Team, the QA Developer:

Writes customized automation programs and scripts, reviews product
requirements documents, creates and executes test cases; creates and
maintains test data; records test results; tracks and creates various
reports and metrics. 
Conducts and assists with the creation, execution and maintenance of
feature, regression and performance tests.  
Develops effective test plans and works with team members to organize
product testing as a part of product development and release cycles. 
Participates in release management process.   
Initiates, monitors and verifies individual bugs and participates in the
bug resolution process.  
Working with team members, participates in the review and selection of
tools and technologies to optimize existing quality practices and
recommend where these might be used to improve quality process and
test automation tools infrastructure.  
Represents and promotes quality engineering and quality assurance methods,
processes and procedures.

* Bachelors Degree in Computer Science.

* 4+ years experience in Quality Assurance.

* Experience in scripting with Perl, Shell, or Python.

* Strong SQL skills, preferably using MySQL.

* Working knowledge of Windows and UNIX/LINUX.
* Experience with automated test tools and defect tracking/management
software.

* Ability to work in a team setting and have a strong attention to detail.
