Role Profile - QA Analyst
Company
As a subsidiary of NetSuite, the industry’s leading provider of cloud-based
financial, ERP, CRM and omnichannel commerce software suites, Venda is one
of the world’s leading innovators of digital eCommerce SAAS solutions,
responsible for sites including Fatface, Boohoo and Tesco. More information
is available at www.venda.com.

Role
The position of QA Analyst is to support the continued success and growth
of Venda by ensuring quality standards are met during the delivery of
changes to the company’s eCommerce Platform.

The successful applicant will be responsible for recreating, documenting
and verifying client-specific issues to facilitate subsequent code fix
development and retesting, as well as helping the QA team continuously
improve the service it provides to internal and external customers.
Excellent written and verbal communication skills are required; experience
with browser automation (e.g. Selenium Webdriver) and programming languages
like Java and Ruby are desirable.

Required skills:
Responsibilities
• Execution of functional (browser-based, API and middleware) testing of
the core platform.
• Ensure that client requirements and quality standards are met.
• Own and track defects and fixes.
• Be proactive in working with developers and other staff in order to
ensure efficient progress of client issues.
• Provide clear test result reports for stakeholders on time.
• Write and modify test scripts and test data according to business
demands.
• Analyse and interpret technical data/reports generated by automated
testing tools.
• Provide weekly reporting on personal progress and achievements against
company/team/personal objectives.
• Ensure continuous learning and personal development.
• Contribute to project, team, and departmental meetings.

Desired skills:
Desirable Skills
• 5+ years of QA/testing experience, ideally with eCommerce (browser-based)
and
middleware (SOAP/REST/API) technologies.
• Experience using Atlassian Confluence (collaborative documentation) and
JIRA (issue tracking) essential.
• Technical aptitude, awareness and knowledge of QA methodologies,
processes and roles.
• Ability to communicate (written and verbal) with both technical and
non-technical teams.
• Ability to use Linux command line utilities.
• Experience with test case management, defect tracking & bug management
tools.
• Experience with programming languages Ruby, Java & Perl and test
automation tools (e.g. Webdriver) desired.

Personal Qualities
• Excellent attention to detail and a passion for ensuring quality.
• Highly self-motivated and proactive.
• Enthusiasm for experimenting with new techniques to help improve and
advance
processes.
• Ability to multitask and work under pressure and deadlines.
• Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and able to accept rapidly
changing business priorities.
