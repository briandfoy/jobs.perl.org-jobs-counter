The Engineer in the Data Operations team optimizes and maintains the daily
process for capturing and processing all revenue and analytic data from the
company's worldwide operations. Primary focus is given to maintaining high
service levels. Team members are also responsible for accuracy of data
loads and resolving any database or system issues in a timely manner. This
position requires all team members to be on-call approximately 4
days/nights per month.

Candidates will also be expected to understand how applications interact
with hardware systems and be able to evaluate new hardware/software
solutions. The main function of the position is to troubleshoot and correct
problems with processes and data and find ways to optimize and improve the
performance and reliability of existing processes, generally through
incremental updates.

Additionally:

* The nature of the environment requires a high level of versatility,
adaptability and well-developed diagnostic and trouble-shooting skills.
* The Data Operations Engineer will potentially be required to assist in
timely identification and correction of issues impacting the business in
significant ways. 
* They will need to assist in the planning, deployment and launching of new
functionality and configurations with extremely minimal disruption to
business operations.

NOTE: this is not a development position, though strong development skills
and experience will aide a successful candidate greatly.

Responsibilities:

Help ensure continuous, 24X7X365 operation of our data processing.

Responsible for working with and coordinating the necessary development
