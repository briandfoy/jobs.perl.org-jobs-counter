System Reliability Engineer

Yahoo! Search Marketing, a division of Yahoo! Inc., offers essential
marketing services for companies doing business online. The group's
search-based products and tools help businesses connect with highly
motivated customers. 

We are seeking a senior system reliability engineer to manage the dynamic
Yahoo! Search Marketing enterprise wide software application suite. Yahoo!
Search Marketing Software Operations Engineers work on a team dedicated to
addressing application performance, security, and stability issues.
Engineers work in a cross-functional environment including development,
networking, hosting systems, database, application servers and web serving.
Knowledge and understanding of each layer, especially software design, is a
critical factor for success.

Responsibilities include the following:

* Work in a team to categorize, prioritize and resolve software application
issues, dealing with all levels of Yahoo! Search Marketing's corporate and
enterprise infrastructure. 
* Develop appropriate applications architecture that sustains Yahoo! Search
Marketing's high standards of availability and performance. 
* Responsible for working with and coordinating the necessary development
and technical operations teams to solve application performance, security,
and stability issues. 
* Function as 3rd tier escalation team for enterprise application 
issues and manage the resolution of issues.

Requirements include the following:

* Minimum 6 years demonstrated software development and/or architecture
experience.
* Excellent development skills and experience in OO Perl, C, and C++. 
* Detailed knowledge tuning and instrumenting Apache and mod_perl.
* In-depth understanding of TCP/IP networking and troubleshooting,
networking architecture and design.
* Excellent knowledge of modern software systems engineering and experience
in building and operating distributed component-based software solutions.
* Experience with data management, relational and object-oriented data
systems required. 
* Outstanding interpersonal and communication skills. 
* Strong research, investigation and evaluation skills.

Highly desirable qualifications include the following:

* Experience with web services.
* Unix System administration experience or equivalent knowledge (Solaris,
Linux, BSD).
* Experience with network programming and IPC.
* Oracle and MySQL experience.
* Consulting experience.
* Experience with successful implementations of complex, highly scalable
and highly available systems in a multi-tier environment.
* Technical or business operations experience in a 24x7 environment.
