Job title: Perl developer / security engineer

Company name: Virus Bulletin

Location: Abingdon, Oxfordshire, UK

Terms of employment: Salaried employee

Hours: Full time

Onsite: yes

Description:
This is a fantastic opportunity to play a role in all areas Virus Bulletin
is involved with, including the development of the Virus Bulletin website
and the company’s growing anti-malware and anti-spam certification testing.


Part of this role involves working on the look and feel of our website as
well as the back-end programming and maintenance. You will have full
responsibility for the functioning of the website (with content contributed
by other members of the team), ensuring a fully functioning, frequently
updated and contemporary commercial website that serves to promote Virus
Bulletin’s products and services.

The other part of the role will be to work alongside the rest of our test
team, contributing to the design, implementation and operation of a wide
range of security testing programmes and related back-end systems.

Ideally, you will have prior experience of running a website or large
projects in Perl or another scripting language. You’ll have Unix/Linux
systems administration skills and will be able to demonstrate a good
understanding of web programming security issues. You’ll also have the
ability to pick up new skills quickly and will enjoy the opportunity for
learning that this position presents.

This is an opportunity to gain new skills and experience. Working within
the small Virus Bulletin team, you will have exposure to the whole business
as well as the opportunity to progress your career as you contribute to our
continued success. You are likely to be a creative individual with the
ability to work methodically to ensure that work is carried out to
schedule.

The position is based at our headquarters in Abingdon, Oxfordshire, UK.

Applicants must be fully authorised to work in the UK.

Main duties:
• Maintain and develop a fresh and contemporary look and feel to the Virus
Bulletin website
• Suggest and implement ideas to enhance and extend the current
functionality of the website
• Implement proactive methods to increase traffic to the website, including
new functions and features
• Maintain a fully automated subscription service including direct online
payment, automated delivery of subscription and automated delivery of
standard communication
• Work on projects in Object Oriented Perl in a Unix/Apache environment
• Write documentation for the website and be responsible for the
troubleshooting, fixing and mitigating of faults
• Ensure the quality of the code base with respect to coding style,
security and reliability
• Contribute to VB's range of testing programmes, including design,
implementation and troubleshooting of tests, test frameworks and test
automation as well as hands-on testing, processing and interpretation of
test results, and liaising with product developers
• Contribute to the implementation and maintenance of back-end services
supporting tests, including sample gathering, classification and storage
systems

Required skills:
Experience of the following is essential:
• Use of Perl of another scripting language
• Use of HTML and CSS
• Experience of running a website
• Good working knowledge of XML
• Knowledge of JavaScript/AJAX
• Unix/Linux systems administration
• Administering different operating systems, including mobile
• Good working knowledge of database programming e.g. MySQL
• Project management

Desired skills:
• Web design experience
• Knowledge of SMTP and HTTP
• Knowledge of security products
• Experience of running a mail server
• Excellent verbal and written communication skills
