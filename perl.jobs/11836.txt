Description:
Tower Research Capital LLC, a high-frequency proprietary trading firm
founded in 1998, seeks computer programmers to develop our in-house systems
and tools, including systems for PnL reconciliation and HR data management.
These systems and tools will be used by internal teams to streamline
processes, organize data, and improve internal communication. This role
offers exposure to a broad range of technologies and the opportunity to
create solutions to challenging problems.


Responsibilities

As an Information Systems Developer, you will develop tools that are agile
and adapt to quickly evolving business requirements. Your responsibilities
may include:

- Designing, building, and maintaining information systems used in the
company’s day-to-day operations
- Working closely with the production support group to automate and
streamlines their processes
- Developing back office and risk management systems for the firm
- Collaborating with other technical team members to modify existing tools 



Qualifications

Successful candidates will have a degree in computer science. Experience
with information management platforms, such as Sharepoint, or with web
application frameworks, such at Ruby on Rails or Django, is a requirement.
Additional requirements include:

- Expertise in a core programming language such as C++, C#, or Java
- Experience with a scripting language such as Python (preferred), Perl, or
bash
- Experience with large, complex databases
- An outstanding eye for detail: you will be working extensively with daily
trade and PnL reconciliation
- Basic Linux skills and knowledge of shell-scripts (strongly preferred)
- Fluency in English 

Additionally, we are seeking someone who is:

- Able to work both independently and in groups
- Committed to following best practices
- Keenly interested in developing technologies
- Enthusiastic about test-driven development 

Financial experience is not required.


Benefits

Tower’s main office and garden roofdeck are located in TriBeCa, a
neighborhood in downtown Manhattan. While we work hard, Tower’s
cubicle-free workplace, jeans-clad workforce, and well-stocked kitchens
reflect the premium the firm places on quality of life. Benefits include:

- Competitive salary
- 401(k) with company matching
- Five weeks of paid vacation per year plus nine paid holidays
- Free breakfast, lunch, and snacks on a daily basis
- Free gym membership
- Free tickets to New York events, including the US Open and TriBeCa Film
Festival
