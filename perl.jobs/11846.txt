Description:
The Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute is a world leader in genomic research,
with an expanding scientific programme dedicated to understanding gene
function in health & disease. 

The Genome Analysis Production Informatics team provide data analysis and
quality control for faculty projects. Support extends to microarray-based
assays for genotyping and gene expression, as well as applications using
high throughput sequencing data: sequence capture, RNAseq and ChIPseq.

We require a developer to work with existing team members to write and
support high throughput, automated analysis pipelines. Additionally,
opportunities will be given to code on other projects within the team
encompassing LIMS development. The predominant language in the group is
Perl. Ruby and R are used also. Training will be offered, where
appropriate.

Required skills:
Essential Skills
Candidates must have strong Perl programming skills, a good understanding
of the software development process and detailed knowledge of relational
database systems. Experience of researching and using/supporting other
programs and packages is essential, as is the ability to study and pick up
new technical skills. Candidates must have experience of handling
biological/genomic data and the ability to liaise with scientists to
establish and discuss specifications.

Desired skills:
Ideal Skills
Experience of statistical computing in R would be advantageous, as would
experience of Ruby and knowledge of SQL, object-relational mappers and
platform LSF.
