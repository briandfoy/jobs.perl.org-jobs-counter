Description:
Summary of Position:
The Senior Application Developer contributes to the development of web
portal interfaces that allow medical care professionals, systems
administrators and technical support staff to securely communicate with
other care providers, and interact with longitudinal health records. These
are integrated with other applications in the CareAlign Suite, a
cross-organizational system for sharing medical records. Daily activities
include working with an Agile scrum team of other engineers to design,
develop, test and maintain software components including user interfaces
and databases. The Senior Application Developer is seen as a resource of
technical subject matter for other engineers on the team and within the
company.

This position can be onsite or a remote working position.  ICA offers an
exceptional benefits package and corporate culture.  Excellent work/life
balance atmosphere and for an onsite working preference, we offer free
parking and are located in the beautiful West End / Midtown district of
Nashville.

Required skills:

•	Four-year college degree in computer science, related scientific
degree or an equivalent combination of education and related experience in
