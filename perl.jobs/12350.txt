Description:
The Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute is a world leader in the use of
large-scale sequencing, informatics and analysis of genetic variation. We
use sequence data to further our understanding of infectious disease and
generate resources of lasting value to biomedical research.

We have an exciting position available for an experienced PERL web
developer to work as part of the Sanger Institute Core Software Services
Team.  The Institute has many and varied biological and sequencing
pipelines that depend upon core software repositories, database and web
service provision.  These resources are used by both Genome Campus
scientific staff and external scientific researchers via our public
websites.

The role is to support the Sanger Institute’s Core-Bioinformatics services,
satisfying current and future requirements for scientific software and
databases.  To achieve this the role encompasses a number of areas:-
•develop and maintain specialized sequence resources and the maintenance of
project-specific data 
•support for Institute staff both in terms of assistance and training 
•contribute to ongoing integration of Core-Bioinformatics resources into
the main internal and external Sanger Institute websites 
•provision of webservices and scripts for Core-Bioinformatic resources 
•extension of Sanger Institute hosted Blast database provision with a view
to extend the functionality and features of Blast services 
•develop a Blast webservice 
•provide access to external biological resources through the acquisition
and mirroring of complex biological databases by the provision of user
friendly interfaces. 
•ensure the Sanger Institute’s suite of Core-Bioinformatics tools reflect
new trends in functionality and biomedical research
