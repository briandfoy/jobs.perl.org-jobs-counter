The Software Engineer will be responsible for designing, developing, and

Title:	Software Engineer for High-Volume Data Warehouse
Company: Ticketmaster
Team: Data Warehousing Team

Job Functions:

Specifically, the software engineer will be responsible for development and
support of custom ETL (Extract, Transform, and Load) processes for
Ticketmaster’s Data Warehousing projects.  The software engineer will also
research, analyze and prepare logical and physical data models for the new
applications and optimize the data structures to enhance data load times
and end-user data access response times.  The software engineer will be
responsible for creating processes to ensure the data quality of the
information collected.

The role requires carrying a pager/cell phone and provide on-call after
hours support.

It would be a plus if the engineer has previous experience working in a
consumer transactions processing environment.
