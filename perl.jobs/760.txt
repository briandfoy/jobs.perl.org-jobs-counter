Description:
Overall purpose of job

The web technical author is an integral member of the ALARIS web site
project team, who are charged with the task of developing and continuous
improvement of a number of web-sites that Alaris International will be
introducing, to meet the needs of our international business. As a member
of the iTEAM (International Technical and Marketing communications team),
the role consists of a broad involvement in all aspects of the company
business. This will include cross-functional teamwork with Quality &
Regulatory (QA/RA), Operations, Marketing, Disposables, Clinical and R & D
Departments.  Responsible for creating and amending the International web
sites and providing technical support, as required for the SAP group.


Key duties and responsibilities

Working with the lead programmer, graphics artists and marketing team, the
web technician will be responsible for building and maintaining the
framework of the sites. Assisting in the writing the code for the web
sites, which are XHTML based.
Having a proactive active approach the technician will be expected to make
suggestions regarding programmatic answers to issues of users interface and
information design. Will be involved with the development and documentation
of all related procedures including the writing of Good Practice
Guidelines.

As required, the role will also provide technical support in the form of
XML & webs transaction tools for the SAP group, which may occasionally
require immediate responses, which could be outside of the normal business
hours.

Start March 2003.

Required skills:
Required education, languages and training

* Education to A Level standard in a variety of subjects, including English

* Bsc hons in related subject would an advantage.


Essential “business focused” and “interpersonal” competencies

* Apache web server administration and configuration.
* Familiar with range of Internet protocols: HTTP/CGI/SSH/SSL.
* A range of programming skills including Perl and XSLT. 
* Proven skills of the XML technology family i.e. XHTML, XML, XSLT and DTD
* Good understanding of web site architecture and implementation.
* A team player willing to become an integral part of a continually
improving environment and willingness to support others in terms of their
role. 
* Eye for detail and high quality output.
* Strong verbal and written communication skills.
* Self-motivated with ability to work with limited supervision.
* Willingness to challenge the status quo.

Applicant must be able to work on-site in the UK.

Desired skills:
* Good Unix/Linux system skills, RedHat Linux preferred.
* Knowledge/experience with MySQL or SQLite (or similar) would be an
advantage
* Certified status e.g. RedHat Linux certified Technician would be an
advantage
* Additional skills and experience as an illustrator and graphics design
would be an advantage.
* Previous experience in a similar position with a keen interest in web
site technologies 

Occasional travel on assignments to other countries maybe required.
