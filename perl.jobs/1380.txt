Barclays Global Investors (BGI), one of the world’s largest institutional
investors with over $1 trillion under management, is looking for a Systems
Developer to work within the Active Data Group developing software to
support BGI’s quantitative investing methodologies. This position will
liaise with researchers, portfolio managers, traders and the market data
team in order to systemize information and build it into BGI’s active
investment models.

BGI is the market leader in active portfolio investment. We manage
portfolios for institutional clients around the globe, and have active
investment models for all developed world equity markets as well as fixed
income, currency and global markets strategies. The ideal candidate will
have an eagerness and desire to work with quantitative financial investment
models. The successful candidate will be working closely with researchers
and portfolio managers, helping them capture their investment insights in a
systematic fashion and maintaining these systems for optimal investment
performance.

·Design and build software to facilitate distribution of data into all of

risk, operations)
·Provide production support to the alpha-generation investments process
·Provide production support for internal group processes
·Work within a global team environment to develop systems for use in all
regions.
