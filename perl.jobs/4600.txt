###Responsibilities### 

* Define system, quality and analysis on systems, networks, and
applications, translating requirements into functional specifications and
effective processes. 
* Provide second-tier oncall support for production services.

validation of new versions.

consultation and strategic direction in systems and hosting technology.

agreed-upon timeframe. 
* Assist in the development and maintenance of security best practices. 
* Own projects for product changes and improvements. 
* Develop tools and scripts to assist with routine maintenance tasks. 
* Mentor the Site Operations team in the usage of tools and scripts and
work with them to develop and deliver optimal solutions for service
recovery and business resumption. 
* Provide recommendations for scalability and performance improvement in
selected areas of discipline. 


Required skills:
* 5+ years of systems administration experience in a large scale production

* 3-5 years scripting with shell, perl, or other languages
