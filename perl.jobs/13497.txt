Job Description-Overview :

TUSK, the Tufts University Sciences Knowledgbase, is a comprehensive open
source educational system serving health sciences graduate education.
Started in 1995, TUSK is now used in 6 US medical schools and 11
universities in the developing world. As part of the USAID funded REPOND
project in conjunction with the Cummings School of Veterinary Medicine, the
Office of Technology for Learning in the Health Sciences, which produces
TUSK is helping universities to respond to emerging pandemic threat. TUSK
is in the process of becoming internationalized to be used in the
Democratic Republic of the Congo.

The Programmer responsibilities will involve:

    *Creating new applications, updating existing ones and determining how
to make separate applications work together.
    *The entire development cycle to create applications within the
framework which will involve requirement gathering, coding, testing, and
releasing.
    *Working with user support to troubleshoot problems and assist with
documentation writing.
    *Exploring new technologies and applications as TUSK grows.
