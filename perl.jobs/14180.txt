Description:
ORGANIZATION DESCRIPTION

Solutions for Progress (SfP) is a mission-driven company committed to
assisting low-and moderate-income individuals and families to sustainably
move out of poverty through the practical application of innovative
technology and public policy. Founded in 1992, SfP is one of the first
organizations to become a Pennsylvania-Registered Benefit Corporation and
is also a B Lab-Certified B Corp. SfP’s staff and management represent an
impressive blend of technical, public policy, research, government, and
community organizing expertise. SfP’s primary product is The Benefit Bank®
(TBB™) online service whose unique civic engagement model and expert
systems technology is revolutionizing how low- and moderate-income
households are able to access federal and state tax credits, student
financial aid, and government benefits. The service creates efficiency by
using a single online platform to assess eligibility and generate/file
applications for
over 20 government benefits and refundable tax credits. Over the last 7
years, TBB has returned over $1.2 billion in benefits and tax refunds to
more than 500,000 people in 10 states.

POSITION SUMMARY
Reporting to the Chief Technology Officer, the IT Services Manager is
responsible for SfP’s technology infrastructure including all production,
marketing and development environments, individual, group and office
support systems, and voice and data communication systems. Whether provided
directly by SfP, hosted by a third party or fully outsourced, the IT
Services Manager is accountable for their overall implementation, technical
coordination, and maintenance.

RESPONSIBILITIES
•	Supervision, evaluation, review and development of IT staff,
including assignment of projects, duties, and responsibilities
•	Establishment and maintenance of an operational environment that is
responsive to the needs of customers (both internal and external) and keeps
SfP management appropriately informed of the status of systems and
resolution of problems
•	Administration of existing servers, network and software services
•	Development and deployment of new services and features
•	Coordination with Software Engineering team to maintain and improve
the production environment for SfP services

Required skills:
REQUIRED SKILLS & EXPERIENCE
•	Experience managing technology staff in a customer service
environment
•	Strong Linux system configuration and administration (Debian
preferred)
•	Familiarity with at least one scripting language such as Bash,
Perl, Python, Ruby, etc. (Bash and Perl preferred)
•	Knowledge of networking configuration and troubleshooting
technologies like DHCP, DNS, firewall rules and routing
•	Experience with a web application development and production and
supporting technologies (i.e. LAMP, SSL certificates, version control, load
balancing, SSH, etc.)
•	Knowledge of backup systems, system monitoring
•	Experience with configuration management systems a plus

Desired skills:
PREFERRED QUALITIES
•	Demonstrated commitment to social justice and anti-poverty issues
•	Excellent written and oral communication skills
•	Ability to work well both independently and collaboratively
•	Flexibility and willingness to work on a wide range of projects.
