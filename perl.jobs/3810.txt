Amazon.co.uk Seeks Bright & Talented Software Development Engineers

Amazon.co.uk Ltd is now looking for talented, experienced and motivated
Software Development Engineers to work within Amazon.com's Catalogue
Software team.
We are therefore organising an Amazon Recruitment Event for the 27th/28th
February in Manchester & London. To obtain more detailed information and

Christina Krainz at ckrainz@amazon.co.uk

Based at our UK headquarters, you will join a team whose software systems
drive the expansion of Amazon's retail selection. Your work will improve
the efficiency and stability of those systems, and your innovations will
drive ongoing improvements to the quality and accuracy of Amazon's
catalogues, across our websites worldwide.

We are looking for people who have built large and complex systems. We are
looking for people who love to write code to solve problems, love to see
their solutions in use, and demonstrate a passion for testing and
continuous improvement.

If you have delivered robust, mission-critical applications using C/C++,
Perl or Java on UNIX, we want to talk to you. Successful applicants must be
articulate, flexible, hard-working, self-directed, smart, and must have
several years of experience, with a degree in computer science, a related
degree, or equivalent experience.

* You will participate in the continuous improvement of our software

on-call rota.
