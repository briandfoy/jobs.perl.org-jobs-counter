Description:
Under general supervision the Software Developer II maintains moderately
complex code of current products, including resolution of identified bugs;
and fulfills requests and addresses functionality questions from the
sales/client services team. In addition, the SEII enhances the
functionality of our current product, or assists in the development of a
new product. While each SEII is assigned to a specific Product Line, (s)he
may also cross product lines at various times, or be assigned to a general
IT project. The assignments of tasks will come from management, and time
expectations and priorities will also be provided.

 
Essential Duties & Responsibilities 
This section is not intended to be an exhaustive list of all duties
required in this position. 
• Maintain current CGI / web applications by modifying the code to enhance
the current functionality of our product.
• Develop solutions by analyzing the needs and requests of our advertisers,
executive staff and business development team.
• Debug current CGI / web applications to resolve any issues within the
code.
• Develop SQL queries to provide data requested from various people within
the organization.
• Improve the speed and operation of our current features while monitoring
and maintaining a minimum load to our system.
• Adhere to all process requirements, including adding appropriate
information to IT Request Log, to ensure communication on all outstanding
and completed issues.
• Adhere to all Change Management procedures to ensure code deployed to
production is accurate.
• Complete all acceptance-level testing required when modifying code
• Adhere to all Coding Guidelines established by management to ensure
modifications to code are consistent.
• Escalate any concerns or issues with IT Requests assigned to you to
management to resolve prior to working on any request.
• Provide daily status report to management at the end of each day,
including IT Request number, description, summary of work performed and
time spent on task.
• Complete a weekly Timesheet to account for work on any project considered
to be new development.
