Petfinder Overview
Petfinder is an on-line, searchable database of animals that need homes. It
is also a directory of over 9,000 animal shelters and adoption
organizations across the USA, Canada and Mexico. Organizations maintain
their own home pages and available pet database. 
Our mission: to use Internet technology and the resources it can generate
to 1) increase public awareness of the availability of high-quality
adoptable pets and to 2) increase the overall effectiveness of pet adoption
programs across North America to the extent that the euthanasia of
adoptable pets is eliminated.
