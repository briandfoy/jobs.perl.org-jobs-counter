Description:
Sigma Space Corp. is seeking an experienced applications programmer to
support LANCE (Level 1 and Atmosphere Near Real-time Compatible with EOS)
system. LANCE system requires developing new LANCE products and data
applications as well as enhancements to Fire Information for Resource
Management System (FIRMS). 
LANCE delivers a total of 96 Near Real Time (NRT) satellite data products
through FTP. Current and planned developments will further integrate Remote
Sensing, GIS and other technologies to deliver satellite imagery and
derived products in a range of easy to use formats including data
visualizing in a Google like State of the Earth and integrating data in to
NASA's new integrated Earth Data site (refer to
http://earthdata.nasa.gov/).

Responsibilities

<li>Maintain and further develop the LANCE - FIRMS WebGIS applications and
spatial web services including administering the FIRMS databases 
<li>Develop programming solutions to make LANCE data more easily accessible
to users (e.g. Datacasting, mobile phone alerts and GeoRSS feeds for
hazards and disasters) 
<li>Work on improved solutions for data download, map display, analysis,
and visualization - contributing to the Google like State of the Earth and
the NASA Earth Data websites
<li>Work closely with other team members to further develop LANCE products,
including Naval Research Laboratory (NRL) data assimilation product, land
water mask, and burned area products. This will involve developing
programming solutions to automate algorithms and provide solutions for data
download, map display, analysis, and visualization.

Required skills:
<li>Bachelor's degree in computer science or related field. 
<li>Three or more years of experience working in Linux and Windows
environments, along with knowledge of GIS and Remote Sensing concepts.
<li>Three or more years of hands-on programming experience with JAVA, C,
PHP, PERL, JavaScript.
