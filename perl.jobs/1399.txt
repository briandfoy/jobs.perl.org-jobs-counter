Primary Job Responsibilities Senior Systems Administrator (Tools)

This position is responsible for improving the efficiency and uptime for IT
services in a data center environment through the development of tools and
automated processes. You will also provide Linux expertise in support of a
global IT infrastructure ensuring system uptime and daily delivery of
systems to defined SLAs. 

• Provide technical leadership in supporting IT systems (including end user
support, problem resolution, team mentorship and business presentations).
• Build tools and automate processes to manage and support a large (>5000
users) multi-site environment consisting of heterogeneous systems both
local and remote.
• Ongoing administration of Linux systems, including monitoring, proactive
maintenance, and problem resolution.
• Establish and follow policies and procedures for infrastructure
administration and problem resolution. 
• Establish and follow best practices for enterprise monitoring,
troubleshooting and maintenance.
• Provide 7x24 operational support and maintenance for the technical
environment and associated sub-systems.
• Mentor to other technical staff members ensuring sufficient expertise for
all supported technologies.
• Work with key business users to understand the business requirements and
potential solutions within the current architecture.

It is desirable for the candidate to have:

• Experience in a customer-focused technical-support environment.
• Experience with CommuniGate Messaging Servers
• A degree in Computer Science or equivalent programming experience. 
Job Requirements • Direct experience with managing multi-site data center
production environments
• Project planning experience and proven track record of managing data
center migrations
• 5+ years experience with administration of Unix/Linux systems (at least
the last 3+ year with 7x24 production support). In that time, demonstrable
experience in the full spectrum of standard administrative tasks such as
server builds, administration and rollouts.
• 3+ years experience with Perl/Shell scripting. WSH/VBS scripting highly
regarded.
• 2+ years experience with administration of Windows systems.
• 1+ years experience with managing production mail software (e.g.:
Sendmail, CommuniGate, etc..)
• 1+ years experience with administration of networking infrastructure
(e.g. CISCO equipment, F5 load balancers).
• Ability to think logically and analyze application and business process
requirements.
• Demonstrate effective communication (written and verbal) skills.
• Self-driven and be able to work independently with minimal supervision.
