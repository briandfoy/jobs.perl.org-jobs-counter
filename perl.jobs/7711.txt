Overview

Six Apart, home to some of the top Perl developers in the world and a
leader in Web 2.0 products and platforms with over 400M subscribers and a
strong financial position, is seeking a Sr. (Contextual) Ad Engineer. 
 
This role leads the technical aspects of Six Apart’s advertising efforts
across all of its product lines, which include two of the most highly
trafficked sites on the internet.  S/he is responsible for creating and
maintaining an ad serving infrastructure, analyzing the performance of
various types of campaigns, and optimizing revenues from advertising. 
Additionally, s/he works cross-functionally to review and approve
campaigns.  The Senior Advertising Engineer is an interdisciplinary
individual who works closely with others across the company to maximize
advertising revenues while maintaining security and privacy.

Duties

•	Design, deploy and maintain Six Apart’s ad serving infrastructure,
including our own ad server.
•	Develop targeting algorithms and continuously improve ad revenues
using A/B testing protocols.
•	Perform technical evaluation of proposed ad campaigns and perform
any engineering duties necessary to roll them out.
•	Write/deploy web-based reporting tools allowing access to and
analysis of advertising campaigns.
