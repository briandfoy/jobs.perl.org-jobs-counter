Group Overview

Enterprise
Platform Engineering (EPE) is part of the wider Application Services group

unified administration portal, which also spans several other services.

We

clients, the application development teams, in a manner similar to a
managed hosting service provider.  AWP team works closely with Product
Management and the Production Support organization that operate the
platforms we deliver.
