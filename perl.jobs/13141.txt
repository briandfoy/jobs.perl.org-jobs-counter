About InfoArmor
InfoArmor was established in 2007 to help Washington Mutual protect the
identities of its 10 million credit card holders. Today it partners with
businesses and organizations to help their employees, members, and
customers gain control of their personal information and protect and
recover their identities.

InfoArmor offers its high-tech and high-touch privacy management and
identity protection and restoration services to the B2B (business to
business) market place. Our innovative and user-friendly services help
employers and partners easily offer a valuable benefit, enhance their
brand, increase loyalty, and generate revenue, all while providing their
stakeholders improved security and peace of mind. We are proud to work with
some leading companies, including prominent wireless, home security,
insurance, and employee benefit providers.

InfoArmor’s strength has been built by joining our unparalleled identity
monitoring technology with world-class customer care. When someone calls
InfoArmor with a question or problem, the call will not be diverted to an
anonymous worker in a distant call center. It will be handled by one of our
specially trained and certified Privacy Advocates, who serve as personal
case managers working to resolve identity protection issues from start to
finish. In the event of identity theft, a Privacy Advocate assigned to the
case will restore the victim’s identity.
