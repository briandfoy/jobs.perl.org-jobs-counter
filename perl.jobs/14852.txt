Description:
Booking.com is looking for a hands-on Team Lead for one of our back-end
software development teams. As a Team Lead Software Development within
Booking.com you will contribute directly to the success and growth of the
#1 accommodation booking website in the world. You will work on a complex,
large scale, world renowned product and witness real time impact of your
work. We offer an international, result-driven, fun, and dynamic
environment. If scale and complexity excite you, Booking.com is the place
to B!

B.responsible

Our software development strategy is agile and we work in small Scrum teams
of 5-7. We do several rollouts every day, are data-driven and do extensive
A/B testing. Our technical culture derives strongly from our strong ties to
the Perl community. Even if you do not know Perl, Booking’s Perl rock stars
will help you get up to speed in order to be able to start solving real
problems from day one. 

Your main focus as a Team Lead Software Development is leading your team of
software developers to success. To get to know our technical environment
you will start off as a hands-on Software Developer in the first few
months. Your responsibilities will include:
•	Leading a team of 4-5 software developers;
•	Being fully hands-on in terms of writing code and participating in
software development projects;
•	Playing an active role in developing new features for a high
performance, scalable web platform;
•	Working with the team and the product owner to define a prioritized
backlog of stories that the team will tackle during a given sprint;
•	Assessing and communicating how your work impacts business and
other systems; 
•	Monitoring team’s progress and take ownership of removing any
impediments that slow the team down;
•	Mentoring people on the team and help them improve their game;
•	Working closely together with other teams to ensure a stable and
reliable product.

B.skilled

•	You have a minimum of 5 years of hands-on software development
experience; 
•	You have experience with coding in more than one programming
language, e.g. Python, C, C++, C#, Java, Ruby or PHP;
•	You are eager to learn Perl;
•	You are comfortable with being hands-on, i.e. code writing and
problem solving;
•	You are an open source fanatic;
•	You have a sound understanding of programming fundamentals and they
application to highly scalable web based systems;
•	You have led a small team;
•	You have a pragmatic outlook and excellent communication skills.
