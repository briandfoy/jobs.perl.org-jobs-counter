Overview
The Information Systems Engineer will be responsible for successfully
Planning, Managing & executing our Data processing, Data Mapping, Data load
using internally developed ETL techniques, and Production runs & final
client delivery of the projects product, based on the specific requirements
of a project.

Responsibilities:
•	Work with Data Services group & other team members to develop and
enhance department Tools, Scripts & processes
•	Plan & execute the data processing & data delivery phases of the
project while working with the Operations management
•	Perform Data Processing, Mapping, Transformation & loading of the
data into Oracle database, making sure it is available to internal groups
on time and with highest quality
•	Produce interim or final deliverables to the client based on the
requirements, following the guidelines of time and quality and coordinating
delivery of client data to be used during engagements.
•	Develop, run and monitor scripts to move client data into
productions servers and databases.
•	Identify and develop procedures to ensure that data quality and
integrity can be verified at each step, creating reusable procedures and
scripts 
•	Attend strategic planning meetings and act as technical liaison
between internal customers and the Operations team, providing ad hoc
reporting and technical support to internal customers when necessary. 
•	Multi-task across the assigned projects based on the defined
priorities
•	Work closely with the Project Manager to gain an exhaustive
understanding of the project's goals and timelines, as well as expected
data composition.
•	Evaluates the production run objectives and generates a production
run plan, including any necessary scripts, tracking documentation, and
processing instructions.
