Description:
The ACU is a registered charity in the higher education sector. The
successful candidate will be working as part of a small IT team maintaining
and developing several websites, internal and external. Applicants may wish
to look at the following URLs for our sites:-

http://www.marshallscholarship.org
http://www.obhe.ac.uk
http://www.acu.ac.uk
http://www.csfp-online.org

The ideal candidate will be an experienced web developer, who is prepared
for a mix of the interesting and the more mundane, with an eye for detail
and a desire to consistently improve systems. The work will mostly consist
of programming web driven database interfaces, both for public facing sites
and an in house intranet. There will be opportunities to contribute to the
overall direction of development, as well as to experiment with new
skills/technologies.

Required skills:
Perl programming experience, with particular emphasis on use of CGI.pm, and
the DBD/DBI database bindings.

Experience with the use of RDBMSs and the SQL language.

Good knowledge of the Apache web server. Experience with apache modules,
mod_perl, embperl, SSI would all be useful.

Comfortable with hand coding HTML/XHTML, Javascript and CSS.

A commitment to providing browser/platform independence through use of web
standards.

Good knowledge of Linux/Unix shell/command line utilities.

Familiarity with images and simple image manipulation for the web; file
formats and their appropriate usage.

Desired skills:
Experience with PostgreSQL, MySQL and Oracle would all be advantageous.

Any exposure to XML, XSLT, XSL-FO, and FOP would be helpful.

An appreciation of security, both with regard to systems, and storage of
data.

Some understanding of HTTP at a protocol level.
