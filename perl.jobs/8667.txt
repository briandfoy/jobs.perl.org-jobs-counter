The Retail Catalog Team is responsible for redefining how Amazon creates
and manages product information for domestic and international Amazon
retail sites.  Our primary team objective is to increase retail item
selection across all marketplaces whilst at the same time, improve the
efficiency and scalability of this process. Our secondary objective is to
drive the infrastructure providing cross-functional integration of our
systems. The scope of these initiatives requires a high-energy, talented
individual who can manage innovation and execution.  This is a high
visibility position and requires sound decision-making capabilities.
 
The successful candidate will participate in all aspects of the software
development life cycle, from defining strategy and architecture through
deployment and support.  The TPM will be responsible for working with the
development team leaders to further develop and refine the software
development processes, contributing to cross-functional technical and
product expertise, assessing and manage risks, anticipating and resolve
bottlenecks, providing escalation management, anticipating and making
tradeoffs, and balancing the business needs with the technical constraints.
They will credibly communicate with and influence business stakeholders,
software engineers, systems architects, and other functional teams as
necessary. This includes protecting existing good processes, eliminating
inefficient processes and best fitting the Retail Catalog specific process
with stakeholders.
 
The TPM must also demonstrate outstanding ability and commitment to making
Amazon a Great Place to Work. He/she must demonstrate strong skills in
creating an environment that fosters learning, teaching, growth,
risk-taking, innovation and fun.


Required skills:

significant experience or relevant qualification in software development,
program management, and product development is preferred.

Experience with Agile Management (SCRUM, RUP, XP, Perl).
Experience with OO Modeling.
Experience of working on internet, UNIX, Middleware, and database related
projects.
A proven track record of delivering large-scale, highly available systems
and quality software products.
