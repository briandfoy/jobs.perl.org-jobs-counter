About Polyvore Engineering

Each month, Polyvore.com has 13 million unique visitors, 200 million
impressions, and 1.2 million sets created using our virtual styling tool.
Our software engineers develop platforms and tools that harness the
creativity of our user community, empowering expression and enabling

with a broad set of skills who are ready to build, scale, and make a
continual impact. We have a philosophy of doing a few things really well-

About this Job

At Polyvore, we strive to build products that delight people. By design, we
keep things simple on the surface yet go to great lengths to make them run
better under the hood. As a frontend engineer, you will be involved with
the design, implementation, polish, and A/B testing of features and library
utils. You will iterate quickly to improve Polyvore in response to data and
user feedback. You'll also be responsible for frontend performance and code
cleanliness. Your tools will be HTML/JS/CSS on the client and perl/MySQL on
the webserver.
