A high-performing, robust, and secure Linux kernel and OS is crucial to
Akamai's products and services. The Kernel/OS Engineering team is

and OS for robustness, stability and performance. The members of the team
often engage in advanced development projects that promise and deliver a
competitive advantage to the Akamai software platform. Our network of over
50,000 servers located around the world is built upon our customized
operating system and kernel, maintained by this group.
