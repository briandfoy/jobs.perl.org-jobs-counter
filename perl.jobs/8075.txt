1.  Consulting with internal business units in defining technical

2. Continued expansion of our custom built content-management system.
3. Integration with technical partners, fulfilling such mission critical

4.  Insuring the timely delivery of major business and editorial

The environment is a standard perl-based LAMP stack running on CentOs
(Apache 2.052 / MySql 5.0.54 / Perl5.8.8).  Production architecture
consists of one database server and three application servers load-balanced
across a network storage appliance.

Required technical skills include:
1. Expert perl knowledge, including OO perl, regular expressions, list

2. Advanced knowledge of MySQL operations, including  inner/outer joins,
database indexing, schema development, and data optimization and
optimization troubleshooting.
3. Client-side coding, including HTML, css, javascript, and ajax.

jquery, etc) is an asset.
4. HTTP protocols and communications, including custom apache
configuration.	SOAP/REST is desirable, and those candidates with mod_perl
expertise are highly sought. 
5. Unix operation and shell scripting.
6. Version Control (CVS/SVN)
7. Mod_rewrite in addition to mod_perl
8. Upcoming needs such as caching and performance tuning
9. Issue tracking in addition to SCM
