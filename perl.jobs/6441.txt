Company Description

 

My client is part of a group – an Anglo-French company and Europe’s largest
online photo service, with projected turnover of around 40M€ in 2007. The
emphasis is on very high quality customer service and products for which we
have won many awards. The group has technology teams and production
operations in London and Paris. The company is still growing quickly, with
currently over 4m members/800,000 active customers generating thousands of
orders per day.

 

Main Purpose of Job

 

To define and design the web architecture for my client’s e-commerce and
storage systems, providing web technology infrastructure to the group. You
will be an extremely experienced web developer in LAMP technologies (Linux
Apache Mysql Perl) with a proven track record of delivery and productivity.
You will be a hands-on person, very actively involved in development and
excited by technology challenges. You will be comfortable with a senior
position holding much responsibility.

 

Relationships

 

Reporting to the Group CTO, based in London. 
Working with my client’s existing web guru/architect. 
Co-ordination of development activity with the project team (programme
manager and project manager). 
 

The job will include these tasks

 

Designing and refining my client’s web architecture. Learning about their
existing architecture, creating innovative ways to improve it and
presenting them effectively to the business. 
Balancing technology expectations between various departments in two
countries 
Working with technology departments to turn your designs into completed
projects. This will involve both management of/liaison with other
developers and a considerable amount of your own software development
effort. 
Understanding every aspect of my client’s technology systems  - not just
those in your immediate job area – including servers, networks, LANs and
WANs and storage systems to such a level that you will be able to offer
advice to the company on any part of them, and third-line support when
necessary. 
 

Skills are required in these areas

 

Linux – knowledge of Linux systems down to kernel level. 
Perl – at least 5 years experience with a full understanding of object
oriented Perl 
Database design and administration – particularly with MySQL 
Storage systems – in-depth understanding of RAID technologies. 
Network architecture – from ethernet through TCP/IP to HTTP and high-level
protocols 
Working knowledge of languages such as C, C++, Java or .NET. 
Ability to set your own goals and manage your own time effectively 
 

Other requirements

 

The job is based in London. The candidate must be a well organised
individual and clear communicator, comfortable with a senior position with
much responsibility. They must have a track record of delivery and
productivity in a software development environment.

 

Salary £40 - £60,000
