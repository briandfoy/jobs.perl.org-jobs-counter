About WorldWinner

WorldWinner.com, a GSN (formerly The Game Show Network)-affiliated site,
hosts online competitions that intensify casual games with the thrill of
playing for cash and prizes.  Offering popular titles such as Solitaire,
Bejeweled 2, Family Feud, SCRABBLE® Cubes, Wheel of Fortune and Cubis,
WorldWinner puts winning within reach for game enthusiasts of all skill
levels who share a passion for competition and a penchant for fun.  With
WorldWinner, game lovers test themselves and challenge each other through
games that engage, invigorate and relax the mind. 

The company’s broad network of partner sites includes leading global
brands, such as AOL Games, EA-Pogo, Lycos, MSN Games and MyPoints. 
WorldWinner is a controlled subsidiary of Liberty Media Corporation
attributed to the Liberty Entertainment Group (Nasdaq: LMDIA).	


Position Summary

The Web Application Developer will be part of our growing web development
group, and will design and develop web applications which provide the user
experience surrounding our interactive web games.

Primary Responsibilities

•	Design and develop web applications 
•	Unit test all components
•	Create design specifications, installation directions, and
operational documentation
•	Support QA in testing functionality, performance, and failure modes

•	Update requirements, bug, and problem databases
•	Support Operations in instrumentation of applications and
components
•	Works effectively with co-workers, customers, and others by sharing
ideas in a constructive and positive manner
•	Listens to and objectively considers ideas and suggestions from
others
•	Keeps commitments
•	Keeps others informed of work progress, timetables and issues
•	Addresses problems and issues constructively to find mutually
acceptable and practical business solutions
•	Other duties as assigned

Required Skills and Experience

•	BS Computer Science or equivalent work experience
•	3-5 years experience in a fast-paced web development environment
•	Demonstrated ability to write concise, manageable and easy to read
code
•	Proficient in Object Oriented Perl and Mason preferred. Proficiency
in another object oriented programming language and templating system will
be considered
•	Proficient in JavaScript and CSS
•	Experience working with Informix and MySQL databases preferred
•	Experience using version control, requirements and bug tracking
systems 
•	Excellent technical writing and reading 
•	Ability to work in a fast-paced, team-oriented development
environment
•	Experience using Flash and PHP a plus
•	Experience working in a Linux environment preferred
•	Knowledge of Apache 2.0 and ModPerl 2.0 preferred
