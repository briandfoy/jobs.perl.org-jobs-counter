Description:
Who are we?
Live Nation Entertainment is the world’s leading live entertainment and
eCommerce company, comprised of four market leaders: Ticketmaster.com, Live
Nation Concerts, Front Line Management Group and Live Nation Network. 
Ticketmaster.com is the global event ticketing leader and one of the
world’s top five eCommerce sites, with over 26 million monthly unique
visitors.  Live Nation Concerts produces over 20,000 shows annually for
more than 2,000 artists globally.  Front Line is the world’s top artist
management company, representing over 250 artists.  These businesses power
Live Nation Network, the leading provider of entertainment marketing
solutions, enabling over 800 advertisers to tap into the 200 million
consumers Live Nation delivers annually through its live event and digital
platforms. For additional information, visit www.livenation.com/investors.

Who are you?
Passionate and motivated.  Driven, with an entrepreneurial spirit. 
Resourceful, innovative, forward thinking and committed. At Live Nation
Entertainment, our people embrace these qualities, so if this sounds like
you then please read on!  

The Role:
Live Nation Entertainment is currently looking for a Front-End Web
Developer to join a progressive high-energy team in their West Hollywood
headquarters. The ideal candidate will have minimum 2-4 years of experience
in web development and production. Individual must be able to work
independently as well as collaboratively within a team and with other
departments. Must be resourceful and be able to understand the full breadth
of workflow and how specific projects affect the company as a whole.
Individual must be a creative thinker, detail-oriented, confident and able
to express ideas in an articulate, well thought-out manner. Ability to
multi-task and prioritize deadlines is a must.

Essential Functions:
Building, maintaining, and amending HTML/CSS and some basic web graphic
work for ticketmaster.com, Ticketmaster International websites and numerous
client branded websites. Working in a collaborative manner with Product,
Creative, and Development departments to maintain and improve the product
through a template approach to HTML and CSS design.

Required skills:
Qualifications:
•	Strong knowledge of pertinent Web design issues including, but not
limited to: browser/platform and Mail Client compatibility, HTML/XHTML and
CSS
•	Strong understanding of Web/W3C standards and guidelines.
•	Knowledge of current trends in web design techniques and technology
advancements (e.g. Ajax, JavaScript, XML, etc)
•	Strong conceptual thinker, with the ability to take printed design
mock-ups and visualize how to execute in HTML with an attention to
technical requirements and the ability to understand and execute HTML as a
3-dimensional object
•	Knowledge of all standard web graphic optimization techniques and
file extensions and appropriate use of each 
•	Minimum 5-7 years experience in hand coding HTML (candidates
dependant upon WYSIWYG applications or editors will not be considered) and
CSS (both inline and external).  Experience with table-less, CSS heavy
layout, as well as basic html, is a must.
•	Experience working in a collaborative environment with other
graphic designers, back-end developers and project managers
•	Experience with JavaScript 
•	Familiarity with server side scripting and database concepts (PHP,
PERL, MySQL, SQL etc) helpful, but not required.
•	Familiarity/Experience with SVN or other version control systems is
a plus 
•	Experience with computer graphics applications such as Photoshop,
Illustrator, and Image Ready--specifically as they relate to web
production.  Note: we are not looking for graphic or flash designers, but
skills as such are a plus
•	Strong communication skills, with the ability to convey concepts /
requirements to a variety of personalities
•	Working knowledge of at least both Mac and PC platforms and
operating systems required; proficiency with one or both
  Knowledge of HTML 5 a plus
